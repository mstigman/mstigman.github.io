Privacy Policy

This policy applies to all information collected or submitted on KayN’s website and our apps for iPhone and any other devices and platforms.

Information we collect

New KayN accounts are created with an email and password, provided if you choose to use Google or Facebook to login.

Email addresses are only used for logging in, password resets, responding to emails that you initiate, and sending notifications that you request. We don’t send promotional emails.

We store information about the reviews you write, votes you submit. All reviews and votes will also be public on the Ethereum blockchain, currently the Rinkeby fork.

Technical basics

All reviews and votes are stored in servers as well as the blockchain and cannot be removed from either, this is to build trust.

Ads and analytics

KayN’s app collects aggregate, anonymous statistics, such as the percentage of users who use particular features, to improve the app.

No ads are present on KayN

Information usage

We use the information we collect to operate and improve our website, apps, and customer support.

We do not share personal information with outside parties except to the extent necessary to accomplish KayN’s functionality. We may share anonymous, aggregate statistics with outside parties, such as how many write reviews for particular businesses.

We may disclose your information in response to subpoenas, court orders, or other legal requirements; to exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims; to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud or abuse, violations of our policies; or to protect our rights and property.

In the future, we may sell to, buy, merge with, or partner with other businesses. In such transactions, user information may be among the transferred assets.

Security

We implement a variety of security measures to help keep your information secure. Passwords are hashed, not stored, using industry-standard methods (currently scrypt).

Accessing, changing, or deleting information

You may access or change your information or delete your account from the KayN iOS app.

However reviews written and votes sent can never be removed as is the nature of decentralization.

KayN may delete your information at any time and for any reason, such as technical needs, legal concerns, abuse prevention, removal of idle accounts, data loss, or any other reason.

Third-party links and content

KayN displays links and content from third-party services, often used to monitor blockchain activity. These have their own independent privacy policies, and we have no responsibility or liability for their content or activities.

California Online Privacy Protection Act Compliance

We comply with the California Online Privacy Protection Act. We therefore will not distribute your personal information to outside parties without your consent.

Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act Compliance

We never collect or maintain information at our website from those we actually know are under 13, and no part of our website is structured to attract anyone under 13.

Information for European Union Customers

By using KayN and providing your information, you authorize us to collect, use, and store your information outside of the European Union.

International Transfers of Information

Information may be processed, stored, and used outside of the country in which you are located. Data privacy laws vary across jurisdictions, and different laws may be applicable to your data depending on where it is processed, stored, or used.

Your Consent

By using our site or apps, you consent to our privacy policy.

Contacting Us
